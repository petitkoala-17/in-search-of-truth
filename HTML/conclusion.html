Final Thoughts

As we reach the end of this journey through this website together, in hopes you traversed it in chronological order, I want you to take a moment to reflect on everything that was discussed on how each section was built upon the previous. It is essential to consider the ideas that may have been implanted in your mind about "persecution." and the teachings you received saying persecution will come your way. I ask you to think back on our time together: have I ever attempted to hurt you or take any action that would have been detrimental to you? Should the answer be "no", ask yourself then "If for more than a year he has not, why would he start now?". This comes purely from a place of genuine care and concern.

To understand what was going on, I had to do my own research. How then can I stand by and say nothing, knowing all what I have learned from this research? Would you have said nothing to me if the roles were reversed?

"Neutrality is at times a graver sin than belligerence." -Louis D. Brandeis

I firmly believe it is vital for you to have knowledge of all this as you walk your spiritual path.

I want to express that I am open to dialogue. If you want, and feel comfortable, I invite you to discuss your thoughts and feelings. Additionally, even though I was already firmly rooted in my faith, I noticed it grew. To effectively carry out this research and put together this site, I had to reconnect, in my own way with God by means of personal prayer, attending mass and discussing with some Catholic priests at the parish about questions I had about things I found and was unsure of while performing this task. Gladly, they expressed openeness to having you come and discuss this, should you be open to it. Just as you required I be open to coming to your church even once, and hearing what they had to say and interact with the people there.

Again, I am not trying to force you into anything; I am simply presenting my findings. It is entirely up to you to read, reflect, pray, and decide for yourself.

Prayer Request

At this time, I ask that you pray once again. Pray for an open heart to take in and accept the things revealed within this site. Open-mindedness to accept them all as presented, without refusal to see them as they are. Pray then for wisdom and discernment to be able to judge for yourself each aspect fairly, and for God's guidance to help you with this task. Pray for patience to not hasten yourself into rash decisions. Finally, pray for courage to permit you to act as need be.

Thank you for taking the time to read this. Remember that I am here for you, should my help be needed.
