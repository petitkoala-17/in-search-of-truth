A Compilation of my Findings and Personal Thoughts

Welcome

Hello! I hope all is well with you. It makes me glad to know that you decided to follow the link and access this site. Herein, you will find multiple pages, each of them dealing with separate aspects of my findings, as well as personal thoughts on everything going on. I believe that if you were moved to come here and see for yourself what lies within, it was for good reason.

With this first step having been made, I now ask that you please approach and navigate this site with an open mind, to fully read, listen, and understand what is found within it. I also ask that you take time to answer the questions you will find within this site on a piece of paper. This will make it easier to stay on track and reflect on them at the end. Finally, it will be up to you to draw your own conclusions. All I can do, as someone who greatly cares for you, is to bring forth the things I have found.

Prayer Request

Before proceeding, I wish to ask of you to pause here, and make a prayer. I ask that you pray for Open-mindedness, to be able to read, and listen to what is found herein with the willingness to weigh it fairly against any current favored beliefs you have or have been given. Patience, to fully navigate this website, and take in its content without skimming or skipping any parts of it.

Wisdom, to understand the things you will read and hear, to make sound decisions in the end. Hindsight and foresight, to be able to reflect on and understand the patterns which may have already arisen that are mentioned here, and the ability to see any new ones which may present themselves. Discernment, to be able to, based on what is within this site, judge adequately things that are yet to come regarding this current predicament. Guidance, to help with the decisions you will make. Courage, finally, to permit you to take the required actions.
