Final Thoughts

As we reach the end of this journey through this website together, I encourage you to reflect on everything we've discussed and how each section built upon the previous one. It is essential to consider the ideas surrounding "persecution" and the teachings you received about it. Think back on our time together: Have I ever attempted to hurt you or acted detrimentally towards you? If your answer is "no," ask yourself: "If he has not done so for over a year, why would that change now?" My intentions stem from genuine care for you.

To understand what was going on, I had to do my own research. How could I stay silent after what I've learned? Would you have remained silent if our roles were reversed?

"Neutrality is at times a graver sin than belligerence." - Louis D. Brandeis

Your awareness of this knowledge is crucial as you navigate your spiritual path.

I want to express that I am open to dialogue. If you want, and feel comfortable, I invite you to discuss your thoughts and feelings. Additionally, even though I was already firmly rooted in my faith, I noticed it grew. To effectively carry out this research and put together this site, I had to reconnect, in my own way, with God through personal prayer, attending mass, and discussing with some Catholic priests at the parish about questions I had regarding things I found and was unsure of while performing this task. They expressed openness to having you come and discuss this, should you be open to it, just as you required I be open to coming to your church and hearing what they had to say.

Again, I am not trying to force you into anything; I am simply presenting my findings. It is entirely up to you to read, reflect, pray, and decide for yourself.

Prayer Request

At this time, I ask that you pray once again. Pray for an open heart to take in and accept the things revealed within this site. Open-mindedness to accept them all as presented, without refusal to see them as they are. Pray then for wisdom and discernment to be able to judge each aspect fairly, and for God's guidance to help you with this task. Pray for patience to take the time needed for thoughtful decisions. Finally, pray for courage to act as need be.

Thank you for taking the time to read this. Remember that I am here for you, should my help be needed.
